About

Brandon Madrid is a passionate conductor and music educator dedicated to creating transformative musical experiences. In Spring 2025, he founded the SoCal Music Collective with a bold vision: to create a space where musicians could challenge themselves, grow, and ultimately become the best artists they can be.

What began as an opportunity for Brandon to gain conducting experience quickly evolved into a collaborative community built on passion, mentorship, and a shared love for music. The ensemble's debut concert, Legacy, reflected this spirit—honoring the music and leaders who've shaped us while paving the way for new voices to rise.

Through the SoCal Music Collective, Brandon is not only carving out his path as a conductor but building a space where musicians can develop their artistry, collaborate meaningfully, and connect deeply with the rich traditions of wind ensemble performance.

His leadership style emphasizes mentorship, artistic growth, and the belief that music has the power to unite and inspire. Brandon handpicks each member of the ensemble, selecting musicians not only for their technical skill but for their dedication, passion, and collaborative spirit.

Under his direction, the SoCal Music Collective has grown from an idea into a thriving ensemble that draws musicians from across California and beyond—united by a shared commitment to excellence and a love for the art form.
